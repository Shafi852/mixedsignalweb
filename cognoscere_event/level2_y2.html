LEVEL

Joseph and You are hired by automobile Electronics Solutions .ltd to design a parking ticket dispenser.Joseph takes the first hand and decides to simulate his idea before taking up the project into a large scale. He wisely employs an RGB LED to indicate the proximity of the vehicle to the counter and displays the distance of separation in the LCD display. As the vehicle approaches the counter the LED changes its colour from Green to Blue to finally Red. Joseph has done his part and set up the circuit. So hurry up, write the code and save your Job!!!

Rules and Regulations

  1. The event is planned for a span of around 10 days consisting of 4 different levels.
  2. Participants should only attempt the questions assigned to their respective year of study.
  3. There is no filtering in order to enter into the subsequent levels, all the registered students can access all the levels; the aggregate score of the participants will be considered for the final ranking.
  4. There will be a common registration link for all the participants which will be open throughout the event. It is mandatory to register for the event before starting to participate in any of the levels.
  5. .People can start participating from any of the 4 levels.
  6. The link for each of the levels will be open only for a specified period of time. Participants need to submit the work within the time frame.
  7. Submission of the work after the specified time will not be considered for evaluation.
  8. Separate grading will be done for each of the levels. The one who submits first will be awarded more marks.
  9. If there is any difficulty in saving the document, then do take a screen recording of your work and submit it.
  10. The event winners will be asked for an ID verification.
