MEC

Govt. Model Engineering College, Thrikkakara is a premier institute that has made it to the pinnacle of the engineering arena in a span of just 20 years under the guidance IHRD. The college has witnessed record-breaking placements channeled through dynamic corporate scenarios. MEC was featured the 28th among the top 50 Government Engineering Colleges in India. Our vision is to evolve into an academy of excellence to serve the emerging knowledge-based society. Unique in its structure, methods and goals, the college is strongly rooted in a philosophy of training and research that emphasizes the intimate relationships between knowledge and its application and seeks to promote the creation of an ideal society. Our mission is to mould engineers in the field of information and technology to bridge the digital divide.

Mixed Signals

Mixed Signals is the Electronics Association of Govt. Model Engineering College, Kochi. This group is an endeavor of the students of Electronics and Communication Department. Mixed Signals aims at providing a platform for the students to learn, experiment and innovate thus transforming into better Engineers. Various workshops, lectures, competitions are organized by the association as part of its mission to empower students with the latest cutting edge technologies. Our main activities include workshops, events, projects etc. Our main aim is to help students understand electronic concepts and foster an interest in them for circuits and design by holding various competitions and talk shows. We offer help in various projects by conducting workshops which include Raspberry Pi, PIC, etc. Mixed Signals is a club for electronic maniacs and enthusiasts.
